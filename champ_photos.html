How it Works

Just click a category below and browse through the images that you like either by using the left and right arrows or by using the image navigation at the bottom of the page. Once you find an image that you want just simply click your right mouse button and choose "save image as..." That's it. It should now be downloaded into your default download directory. These are large images so please give them a few seconds to load.

Equipment I used:

Camera: Canon 5D Mark III

Lens: Canon EF 70-200mm f/2.8L IS II USM

Accessories: B+W 77mm Kaesemann circular polarizer filter, B+W 77mm clear UV haze filter
