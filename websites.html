About Me

My name is Kevin Ray. I am currently a senior at the Art Institute of Portland working on a Bachelor's Degree in Web Development and Interactive Media. As a web developer/designer, graphic designer, motion graphic designer, production designer, and photographer, and being that the term "Legion" simply means - MANY, I felt the name fitting since I am one man perfecting MANY types of designs. In everything that I design I feel that “simplicity is bliss”. This is why I have become addicted to typography in the last year. I feel typography is one of the easiest ways to create beauty through simplicity. It tells a story in itself; it helps you navigate through whatever space you are in – it is simple and effective. This type of thinking is always in my head. This belief creates the most key piece of design – FUNCTIONALITY. Whether it is a logo, photograph, TV commercial, or a website, the user experience is everything. That is why whenever I start a project I always tell myself – "great design breeds great experiences"

I have personally worked with hundreds of individuals, small businesses, startups and even corporations. I design all of my sites mobile first makeing sure they are fully responsive no matter what device the user is viewing your page on. I am equally skilled at designing corporate websites as well as animated and fun websites.
